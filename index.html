About Me

I was born and raised on the north side of Chicago. I attended the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign where I majored in Global Studies with a focus on the Arab World. After graduating in 2015, I became a nanny. Being a nanny offered me a more flexible schedule and in that time I was able to travel a great deal with one of my favorite travel companions, my mom. After my last recent family moved out west, I pivoted towards another one of my interests: cooking. I got a job as a line cook at Sunda Chicago and worked there until the day the Mayor of Chicago, Lori Lightfoot, shut down all the restaurants due to the pandemic. I spent the pandemic exploring my hobbies and indulging in all kinds of fun recipes and desserts. When we made it to 2021, I decided it was time to pivot to my next adventure which is how I ended up at Coding Temple.
